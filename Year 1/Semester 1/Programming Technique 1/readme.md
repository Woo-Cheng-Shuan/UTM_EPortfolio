## 💭 Reflection

This course provided a strong foundation in problem-solving and programming concepts through the use of the C++ programming language. Throughout the semester, various programming techniques and logical approaches were introduced, enabling solutions to be developed for problems of increasing complexity.

Practical experience was gained through coding exercises and assignments, where programming concepts such as decision-making, loops, functions, arrays, and structured program design were applied. These activities helped strengthen logical thinking, analytical skills, and the ability to approach problems systematically.

In addition, valuable exposure was gained through industry talks and visits, where insights into the professional IT environment were shared by industry practitioners. These experiences provided a better understanding of industry expectations, career opportunities, and the skills required to succeed in the technology field.

Overall, this course served as an important starting point in the study of computer science. A solid foundation in programming and problem-solving was established, which has continued to support learning in more advanced computing and software development subjects.
