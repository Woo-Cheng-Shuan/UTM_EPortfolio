## 💭 Reflection

This course provided a broad introduction to technology and information systems, covering fundamental topics such as computer hardware, software, networking, and digital communication. Through lectures, assignments, and practical activities, a foundational understanding of how technology supports both personal and organizational activities was developed.

Hands-on experience was also gained through PC assembly and installation lab sessions, where the basic components of a computer system and their functions were explored. These activities helped strengthen technical knowledge while providing practical exposure to computer hardware.

One of the most valuable aspects of the course was the opportunity to participate in industry talks and industry visits. Through these activities, insights into the real working environment of the IT industry were gained, along with a better understanding of current technologies, industry expectations, and career opportunities. Valuable advice and experiences shared by industry professionals also provided motivation and guidance for future career planning.

Overall, this course served as an excellent introduction to the field of computing and information technology. The knowledge and exposure gained helped build a strong foundation for future studies while providing an early understanding of the skills and competencies required in the IT industry.
