# 🌱 Year 1 - Building the Foundation

## 📖 Overview

Year 1 marked the beginning of my Computer Science (Data Engineering) journey at UTM. Throughout the year, I was introduced to the core concepts of computing, including programming, system design, statistics, computer architecture, and user-centered development.

## 📚 Semester 1

### Subjects
- Digital Logic
- Discrete Structure
- Programming Technique I
- Technology & Information System (TIS)

### 🎯 Semester Focus
Building fundamental knowledge in programming, logical thinking, and computer systems.

### 💭 Reflection
My first semester helped me adapt to university learning and develop a strong foundation in computing. I learned how to approach problems logically, think critically, and understand the basic technologies behind modern computer systems.

Year 1 provided me with a strong foundation for my future studies in Data Engineering.
