# Database

## Reflection

The Database course introduced me to the principles of storing, organizing, and managing data effectively. I learned how to design databases using Entity Relationship Diagrams (ERD), normalization techniques, and SQL queries. Through practical assignments, I gained hands-on experience in creating tables, managing relationships, and retrieving meaningful information from databases.

One of the most valuable lessons from this course was understanding the importance of proper database design in ensuring data consistency and integrity. The knowledge gained here became an important foundation for later subjects such as Data Engineering and Business Intelligence. Overall, this course enhanced both my technical skills and understanding of data management.
