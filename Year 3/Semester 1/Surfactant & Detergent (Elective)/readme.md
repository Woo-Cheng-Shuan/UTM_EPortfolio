## 💭 Reflection

This elective course provided exposure to the science and applications of surfactants and detergents in everyday life and industrial processes. Through the study of chemical properties, formulation techniques, cleaning mechanisms, and environmental considerations, a broader understanding of the role of chemistry in consumer and industrial products was developed.

The course offered a unique learning experience outside the field of computing, allowing scientific concepts to be explored from a different perspective. Knowledge was gained on how surfactants function, how detergent products are formulated, and how product performance is evaluated.

Overall, this course broadened my academic experience by introducing concepts from another discipline and highlighting the importance of interdisciplinary knowledge in solving real-world problems.
