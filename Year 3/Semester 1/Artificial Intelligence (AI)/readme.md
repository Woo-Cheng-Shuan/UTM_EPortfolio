## 💭 Reflection

This course introduced the fundamental concepts of Artificial Intelligence and how intelligent systems are developed to solve complex problems. Through topics such as machine learning, search algorithms, knowledge representation, and neural networks, a deeper understanding of how AI technologies are applied in real-world scenarios was developed.

Practical activities and assignments provided valuable exposure to AI techniques and model development. The ability to analyze data, identify patterns, and evaluate model performance was strengthened throughout the course. In addition, awareness was gained of both the opportunities and challenges associated with AI technologies.

Overall, this course provided a solid foundation in Artificial Intelligence and enhanced my interest in data-driven technologies, which are highly relevant to the field of Data Engineering.
