## 💭 Reflection

This course provided a comprehensive understanding of how enterprise systems are analyzed, designed, and modeled to support organizational processes. Through the UTHM project, practical experience was gained in gathering requirements, designing system architectures, modeling business processes, and developing system documentation.

The project demonstrated how technology solutions can be aligned with business needs through structured methodologies and enterprise architecture principles. Skills in system analysis, problem-solving, documentation, and stakeholder communication were strengthened throughout the development process.

Overall, this course enhanced my ability to view systems from both technical and business perspectives while providing valuable experience in designing scalable and organized enterprise solutions.
