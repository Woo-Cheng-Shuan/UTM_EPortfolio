# ☁️ Year 3 - Specializing in Data Engineering

## 📖 Overview

Year 3 marked the beginning of my specialization in Data Engineering. This year, I moved beyond traditional software development and focused on data pipelines, cloud platforms, business intelligence, and enterprise systems. Through various projects, industry collaborations, and technical assignments, I gained valuable exposure to how data is managed and utilized in real-world organizations.

### Subjects
- Enterprise Systems Design and Modelling (ESDM)
- Business Intelligence (BI)
- High Performance Data Processing (HPDP)
- Special Topic in Data Engineering (STDE)

Exploring enterprise systems, data analytics, big data processing, and modern data engineering technologies.

### 💭 Reflection
This semester provided me with practical experience in handling large datasets, developing business intelligence solutions, and understanding enterprise business processes. Through projects and industry case studies, I learned how data can be transformed into valuable insights that support decision-making.
