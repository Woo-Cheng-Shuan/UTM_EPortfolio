## 💭 Reflection

This course provided a comprehensive understanding of how data can be transformed into meaningful insights to support business decision-making. Through the use of tools such as Alteryx and Power BI, practical experience was gained in data preparation, transformation, analysis, and visualization.

The flight delay case study project provided an opportunity to work with a large real-world dataset and apply business intelligence techniques to identify trends, operational issues, and potential improvements. The process of developing dashboards and presenting findings strengthened both analytical and data storytelling skills.

Overall, this course improved my ability to extract insights from data and communicate information effectively through visualizations. The knowledge gained has also strengthened my understanding of data-driven decision-making in business environments.
