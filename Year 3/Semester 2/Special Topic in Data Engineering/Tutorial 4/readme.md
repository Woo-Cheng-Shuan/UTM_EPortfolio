## 💭 Reflection

This tutorial demonstrated how AI-assisted tools can be used to support the development of a complete data engineering pipeline. Through the use of tools such as Cursor Agent and Claude, dbt models, configuration files, and project structures were generated based on specified requirements, showcasing the potential of AI in accelerating development workflows.

A deeper understanding was gained of how data engineering processes, including data ingestion, transformation, and loading, are implemented within dbt using staging, intermediate, and mart layers. Observing these concepts in a practical environment helped bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world implementation.

The importance of writing clear and detailed prompts was also highlighted throughout the tutorial, as the quality of the generated output was highly dependent on the instructions provided. Although AI tools were effective in generating code and assisting with debugging, human understanding remained essential for validating results, identifying issues, and ensuring accuracy.

Overall, this tutorial provided valuable insight into how AI can enhance modern data engineering workflows by improving efficiency while still requiring critical thinking and technical knowledge from developers.
