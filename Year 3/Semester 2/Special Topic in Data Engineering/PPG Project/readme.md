## Reflection

Through this project, valuable knowledge and practical experience in data engineering were gained, particularly in designing and implementing an end-to-end data pipeline using Microsoft Azure services and Power BI. A better understanding of how raw business data can be transformed into meaningful insights through data cleaning, transformation, modeling, and visualization was developed.

The project also provided exposure to real-world inventory management challenges, including excess inventory, expired materials, stock shortages, and their impact on production and customer orders. In addition to technical skills, teamwork, communication, and problem-solving abilities were strengthened through collaboration with team members throughout the project.

Overall, a deeper appreciation for the role of data engineering in supporting business decision-making was developed, and confidence in applying data engineering concepts to real industry scenarios was significantly improved.
