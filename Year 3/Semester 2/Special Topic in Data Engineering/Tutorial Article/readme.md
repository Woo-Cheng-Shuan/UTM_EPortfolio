## 💭 Reflection

This tutorial provided valuable exposure to the process of designing a Business Intelligence (BI) solution from both business and technical perspectives. Through the case study, a deeper understanding was developed of how business problems can be translated into data-driven solutions using dashboards, data warehouses, and analytical models.

Knowledge was gained in gathering business requirements, identifying user needs through user stories, and designing system components such as use case diagrams, ERD models, system architectures, and galaxy schemas. The tutorial also highlighted the importance of integrating data from multiple departments through ETL processes to create a centralized and reliable source of information.

In addition, a better understanding was developed of how Business Intelligence dashboards can support performance monitoring and decision-making by transforming raw operational data into meaningful insights. The experience also strengthened my skills in data modelling and analytical thinking, which are essential in the field of data engineering.

Overall, this tutorial demonstrated how different data engineering and business intelligence concepts work together to support organizational decision-making and improve business performance.
